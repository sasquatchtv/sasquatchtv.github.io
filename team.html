David DeSimone

Videographer

David DeSimone currently attends Lutheran High school and participates in many extracurricular activities including track, cross-country, and International Travel club. As a runner he competes in the 800 meter dash and the 1600 meter, contributing to his school’s three time consecutive state championships. Inside the classroom, David enjoys engaging in foreign language studies and plans to travel to Costa Rica to live among the culture and native language. Among his passion for school, he also greatly values videography and photography. Since high school he has developed experience in business videoing, which has inspired him to initiate the Sasquatch Project. His abilities include pre-production, equipment expertise, and professional editing using Final Cut Pro. He contributes these skills to the production of Sasquatch Commercials. When it comes to photography, David is driven to capture the perspectives within nature. He has a strong background in business and event photography with a skill set in photo editing through the use of Adobe Lightroom. In addition to nature and business photography, time lapse photography also allows him to explore the essence of nature. In the future, David hopes to attend Colorado State University of Fort Collins to study veterinary medicine. He aspires to explore the field of domestic animal medicine while minoring in foreign language studies. David is a motivated student and an inspired entrepreneur.

Kyle Venberg

Chief Editor

Steve Jobs said, “Great things in business are never done by one person. They're done by a team of people.” This quote holds true when it comes to Kyle Venberg and his role in the Sasquatch Commercial team. As the Chief Editor, not only does he edit and produce the Sasquatch commercials but he also designs the graphics and titles for each commercial using Final Cut Pro X and Motion 5. After the graphics and edited material are assembled, the video is fine tuned and reviewed before final publication. His passion for videography can also be seen on his YouTube Channel, Command Creativity, where he creates tutorials for Apple Professional Software and technology in general. His channel also has a follower base of more than 8,000 individuals. Besides creating content for the channel, Kyle also works at Best Buy as a sales consultant specializing in the computer and digital imaging areas. After graduating from Lutheran High School, Kyle is going to Biola University in La Mirada, California studying Cinema and Media Arts with a minor in Business Administration. With a dream of opening his own production company in the future, Kyle strives to make an impact and produce high quality results with each project. Working in a team is the best way to grow a business as each individual has a unique skillset. Kyle’s portfolio can be seen on his online website and his means for contact can be found on the website as well, please give his online portfolio a look by visiting www.kylevenberg.com.

Paige Dickman

Business Manager

Paige Dickman is an inspiring individual with a passion for business and entrepreneurship. As a senior in High school, Paige has developed a plethora of experience with managing and leading in the business world. Prior to starting Sasquatch Tv, she was hired by her school to develop a commercial for the new EDGE Program that her High School was offering. In addition, Paige manages and directs the filming for the daily video announcements of her school, and has gained many filming and leadership skills in the process. When it comes to credentials, Paige is PC applications certified and has advanced education in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere, Adobe Muse, and Final Cut Pro. In fact, Paige design and produced the Sasquatch Tv website and manages it through Adobe Muse. Paige is also a strong business leader. Due to four years working directly with her father, an entrepreneur, Paige has gained many experiences and knowledge that you can only find in the business world. Thanks to the support and encouragement from her family and friends, Paige was able to reach the goal of producing Sasquatch Tv for her local community.
