What is Sasquatch?

Sasquatch Advertising is a local advertising group based in Parker, Colorado. We offer video production to small, locally-owned businesses in the general Parker area.

After video production, we broadcast the advertisement to our network of monitors that are set up throughout stores in the town. Each monitor plays a businesâ€™ own commercial and a series of other commercials made for other businesses. Sasquatch strives to promote stimulation in the small business environment so that each advertiser can support themselves and help support their fellow businesses by playing their video.

While Sasquatch Advertising is a relatively new business launched locally out of Parker, Colorado, our team has big plans for the future. Expanding our reach to more businesses and growing the advertising program in Parker is our present goal, while reaching out to more towns in the Colorado area is our future vision.

We envision an advertising network that links many businesses together to promote local business growth and community. Establishing a system that promotes fellow business growth is our main objective and linking the town and educating individuals on town happenings is crucial. Sasquatch is a medium for advertising in a local sense, bringing together the community and local businesses. As for the future, Sasquatch is looking to build more networks across more cities and seeks to strengthen the bond between local businesses and the community.
